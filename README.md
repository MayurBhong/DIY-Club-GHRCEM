# 🎨DIY-Club-GHRCEM.
## 🏫 G H Raisoni College of Engineering and Management, Pune


## 📌 About the DIY Club

DIY (Do It Yourself) Club is a creative and skill driven community where students share knowledge, ideas, and resources to build and customize projects independently. The club provides a dedicated platform for students to **showcase their talent, creativity, and innovation**, fostering self-sufficiency and confidence. DIY encourages learning beyond academics through hands-on experiences and creative expression.


## 🚀 Innovate • Collaborate • Elevate

In today’s fast paced and ever evolving world, engineers are expected to be innovative thinkers and creative problem solvers. Employers value individuals who bring fresh perspectives and original ideas. The DIY initiative was introduced to nurture creativity among students and help them grow holistically. Through engaging activities, students develop not only technical skills but also a mindset of innovation, exploration, and collaboration.


## 🎯 Vision and Purpose

DIY is more than an event. It is a continuous journey of **self discovery and personal growth**.

The club focuses on development across three key learning domains:

- 🧠 **Cognitive Domain**  
  Enhances critical thinking, creativity, and problem solving skills.

- ❤️ **Affective Domain**  
  Builds confidence, self expression, motivation, and teamwork.

- ✋ **Psychomotor Domain**  
  Develops practical, hands on skills through creative activities.


## 🎨 Activities and Competitions

DIY Club organizes a wide range of creative and skill based competitions, including:

- ✏️ Sketching  
- 🎨 Painting  
- 🖼️ Collage Making  
- 🏗️ Model Making  
- 📄 Origami  
- 🎭 Face Painting  
- 🌸 Mehendi Art  
- 💅 Nail Art  
- 🍳 Cooking  
- 🧩 And many more creative challenges  

These activities encourage students to explore their interests, learn new techniques, and push creative boundaries.


## 🌱 Learning Outcomes

By participating in DIY Club, students gain:

- Enhanced creativity and innovation skills  
- Practical hands on experience  
- Improved confidence and self expression  
- Strong collaboration and teamwork abilities  
- A mindset of exploration and independent learning
